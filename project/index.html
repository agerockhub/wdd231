Overview

The purpose of StaffManage Pro is to provide a dynamic, lightweight, and user-friendly platform that allows administrators to manage teaching staff information. The system captures, organizes, and retrieves personal and professional staff details, while also tracking current and completed student batches.

The following is a summary of what this system offers you:

  • Course information
  • Current batches
  • Completed batches
  • Teachers profile
  • Add a staff to the system
  • Add a student to the system
