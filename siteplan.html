Website Planning Document

Site Name:

Tesano Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:

Apex Chamber of Commerce unites Tesano businesses, fostering trade, collaboration, and empowerment while driving community development and sustainable growth.

Target Market:

Business owners and consumers within the Tesano community and its surrounding areas who seek growth, collaboration, and community-driven opportunities.

Goals:

  • Create a dynamic trade ecosystem that empowers businesses to collaborate, trade, and grow together.
  • Enhance member engagement by delivering valuable resources, training programs, and networking opportunities.
  • Attract new businesses to Tesano while driving sustainable economic development in the community.
  • Establish the Chamber as a trusted authority and leading voice in local business matters.

Scenarios

  • What are the benefits of joining the Apex Chamber of Commerce?

    Members gain access to trade discounts, networking opportunities, resources, and increased business visibility within the Tesano community.

  • How much does membership cost, and what’s included?

    Membership fees vary by category, with benefits such as discounted services, event access, and promotional opportunities.

  • How do I apply to become a member?

    You can apply online through our membership form or visit our office in Tesano for assistance.

  • What networking events, workshops, or training programs do you organize?

    We host regular networking sessions, business workshops, and training programs designed to foster growth and collaboration.

  • How does the trade ecosystem work, and how do I participate?

    Members trade goods and services among themselves at discounted rates while promoting each other's businesses through the Chamber platform.

  • How does the Chamber contribute to community growth and empowerment?

    We contribute to a collective fund that supports community projects, skill development, and empowerment programs.

  • Can non-business owners (patrons) support or get involved?

    Yes, patrons can support Chamber initiatives, attend events, and contribute to community development projects.

  • How does the Chamber help improve the visibility of local businesses?

    We promote member businesses through events, our website, and community campaigns to strengthen their visibility and credibility.

Color Schema:

Typography

  • Montserrat (for headings and navigation) Why: Bold, clean, and professional with a contemporary look that grabs attention. For:Page titles, section headers, navbar links. Pairing style: Pairs well with softer body fonts like Open Sans or Lora.
  • Lora (for body text) Why: A serif font with a warm, elegant touch—great for long reads and storytelling. For: Paragraphs, article content, descriptions. Pairing style: Adds a graceful, traditional vibe that complements nature and culture themes. 3. Roboto Condensed (for buttons, captions, and subheadings) Why: Compact, easy to read, and great for UI elements where space is limited. For: Button text, photo captions, cards, tour summaries, or pricing labels.
